Pico y Placa Predictor
This application assists you in determining whether a car is permitted to circulate on a particular day and hour within the city of Quito.
based on:
-Schedule: 
  Morning
    06:00 to 09:30
  Afternoon
    16:00 to 20:00
-Day of the week.
-Number of the plate.
